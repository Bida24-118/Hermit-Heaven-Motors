Hermit Heaven motors is one of the largest automobile manufacturers in the Botswana, producing about 6 thousand vehicles per year. This is a company originally founded by Learners Feledi who then passed it to his son Lesego Feledi to grow the legacy.

Following consolidations among many of its business units, the company was name Hermit Heaven motors with the aim to provide fast,confortable and reliable cars to customers. It has assembly plants and distributors across the country, and it owns subsidiaries that produce cars.
